About Me

Biography

Hey there! My name is Marcela and I'm a seventeen year old with a passion for Computer Science. Honduran born and raised, I moved to New York City 1 month shy of my fifteenth birthday, and was later enrolled at George Westinghouse High School and placed in the school's multimedia program, where my love for coding started.

I enjoy making small webpages from scratch, playing around with Javascript, and always taking my code an extra mile.
